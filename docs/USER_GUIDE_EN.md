# SaleTrack User Guide (English)

Welcome to SaleTrack! This guide provides a quick overview of how to use the system based on your role.

## For Salespeople

SaleTrack helps you track your daily attendance, leads, and real estate deals.

### 1. Initial Setup
- You will receive an email invitation from an Admin.
- Click the link in the email to set your personal password.
- Sign in using your email and the password you just created.

### 2. Daily Activities
- Upon logging in, you will land on your **Dashboard**, which provides an overview of your current month's performance.
- To record your daily attendance and metrics, navigate to the **Activity** tab.
- Click **Log Activity** to enter your daily leads, calls, meetings, and attendance status.
- *Note: You can edit your activity logs for the current calendar month.*

### 3. Managing Deals & Units
- To manage your assigned properties and customers, navigate to the **Deals** tab.
- Click **New Deal** to create a deal for a property unit.
- You can update the state of your deals as they progress (e.g., from *Prospecting* to *Negotiation* to *Closed*).
- You can upload photos of the units directly to the deal page.

---

## For Administrators

As an Admin, you have visibility into the entire team's performance and the ability to manage user accounts.

### 1. User Management
- Navigate to the **Users** tab.
- Click **Invite Salesperson** to add a new team member. They will automatically receive an invitation email.
- You can activate or deactivate accounts from this view if a salesperson leaves the team.

### 2. Reporting & Dashboards
- Your **Overview Dashboard** provides aggregated metrics for the entire sales team.
- You can filter the dashboard by date, user, source, and attendance to get granular insights.
- The **Reports** tab allows you to export filtered activity and deal data to CSV for further analysis in Excel or other tools.

### 3. Auditing & Corrections
- While salespeople can only edit their recent activities, you have the ability to edit any past activity or deal record if a correction is necessary.
- *Note: All admin corrections are logged in an immutable audit trail for compliance.*
